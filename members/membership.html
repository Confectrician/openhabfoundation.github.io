Become a Member

The current smarthome market suffers under the commercial interests and competition of big companies, which are fighting for customers and try to force their proprietary ecosystems. This leads to a highly fragmented market with vendor lock-ins, making participation and contribution of the public a true challenge with barriers.

We highly believe that smart homes must not be closed or restricted, but that everybody must have the freedom to participate, modify and enhance it.

The openHAB Foundation e.V. was founded to boost innovation and to make smart home technology available for everybody by empowering individuals and organizations.

The Foundation is the central place for supporters which want to spread the word about openHAB and want to help becoming it a respected alternative to commercial solutions.
